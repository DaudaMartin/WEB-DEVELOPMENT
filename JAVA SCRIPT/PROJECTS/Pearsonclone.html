Our Company

We add life to a lifetime of learning.

We’re the world’s leading learning company, dedicated to helping
everyone realise the life they imagine.

Our purpose is to add life
to a lifetime of learning.

At Pearson, our purpose is simple; to add life to a lifetime of learning. We believe that every learning opportunity is a chance for a personal breakthrough. That's why our c.20,000 Pearson employees are committed to creating vibrant and enriching learning experiences designed for real-life impact. We are the world's leading learning company, serving customers in nearly 200 countries with digital content, assessments, qualifications, and data. For us, learning isn't just what we do. It's who we are.

Our vision to help everyone to realise the life they imagine through learning.

We use our knowledge, experience and technology to deliver the world’s best learning products to more people around the world than ever before. We do it because we are here to help people achieve their potential because we know that when we demonstrate the value of learning, we demonstrate the value of our business and transform lives.

We have an integrated business to support customers through their learning journey

Our digital-first business model puts our customers and consumers at the heart of everything we do, enabling us to deliver the best products and learning outcomes to more people than ever before. It drives our success and enables us to grow with – and adapt to – a rapidly changing learning market.

We're structured for growth

Our five integrated businesses support customers through their lifelong learning journeys - Assessment & Qualifications, Virtual Learning, English Language Learning, Workforce Skills and Higher Education.

Leadership team

Our leadership team come together from a variety of backgrounds and areas of expertise. They are united by their belief in learning as a force for positive change for individuals and for society, and by their commitment to ensuring that Pearson continues to meet society’s rapidly changing needs as the world’s leading learning company
